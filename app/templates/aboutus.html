About Me

My name is Monica Tong. I'm the girl on the right, my sister is on the left. I'm a senior in High School, and I'm building this website as part of my capstone class. We chose a topic we wanted to research (mine was school education) and to build a website based off of the topic chosen.

Purpose

My intended purpose is to provide an efficient means for parents and school staff to communicate with each other, and to also provide sufficient resources and support for low-income parents.

Parents interactivity: Be updated on your child's behavior and grades, communicate with teachers, catch up on school news, and check out our resources page which includes volunteer opportunities.

Teachers interactivity: Input and sync progress reports, communicate with parents, post any volunteer opportunities you would like to post.
